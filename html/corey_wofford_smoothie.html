Green Smoothie

Description

This is a tasty treat of blended fruit and greens!

Ingredients

  • bunch of greens like kale, lettuce or spinach
  • 2 peeledvery ripe bananas
  • 2 peeled oranges
  • crushed ice

Directions

  1. Throw everything into a Vitamix blender.
  2. Add water until the blades are covered by at least an inch of water level.
  3. Turn the Vitamix on at its lowest setting.
  4. Gradually increase speed until blending at high speed.
  5. Continue to blend until there are no visible fragments of green (complete pulverization).
  6. Gradually decrease the Vitamix speed..
  7. Serve and enjoy!
